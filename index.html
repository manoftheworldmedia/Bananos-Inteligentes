04 FAQ

Questions, answered.

Does it replace our existing systems?

No. Bananos Inteligentes sits over the data you already collect — yield, weather, rot, spray and soil — and unifies it into a single intelligence layer. No migration required; it adds intelligence on top of what's already there.

How long does setup take?

Pilot setup runs in weeks, not months. We start in the field with the questions your team is already asking, then connect the data sources behind them — so value arrives early.

How does it handle Black Sigatoka?

It correlates weather patterns with historical rot and spray efficacy to get ahead of disease pressure — telling teams not just when to spray, but what. Black Sigatoka moves from constant emergency to manageable variable.

Who owns the data?

You do. Operators own their operational data. The intelligence layer runs over the data you already collect — it does not extract or resell it.

What languages does Banana Chat support?

Spanish and English. Any team member — agronomy, logistics, sales or research — can ask in their own words and pull from the same source of truth.
